About

This is the chess club for Centennial. It's a high school chess club with connections to other schools through SD43 Junior Chess Association and BC Junior Chess Association. We aim to improve people's chess skills and help people like chess more.

Also, check out the SD43 Junior Chess Association and the BC Junior Chess Association! There are lots of tournaments you can join.
